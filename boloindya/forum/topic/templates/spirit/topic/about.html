About Us

Bolo Indya is a Platform on the mission to empower the language internet users with knowledge - Enable them to share views, gain and share knowledge through audio and video in their local Languages.

It's an audio and video platform to ask questions and connect with people who contribute unique insights and quality answers.

Bolo Indya enables people to select language of their choice, ask question through audio/video on any topic of interest, and get its answer through audio and video - and learn from each other - making internet world the more valuable place to spend time, promoting an eco system which encourages healthy debates to arrive at definitive answers.

Let us together unlock the knowledge of 100 crore Indians, bring together different perspectives, understand interest areas better and make Internet a better world to live in, without giving away our love for our native languages.
